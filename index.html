My Story

A Finance Professional
Built for Impact

My journey into finance began with a simple conviction: numbers are not just data — they are the language of business decisions. Growing up in Kenya, I watched how access to smart financial tools — or the lack thereof — could determine whether businesses thrived or stalled.

That conviction drove me to KCA University, where I earned my Bachelor of Commerce in Finance, graduating in November 2024. During my studies, I didn't wait for opportunity — I created it, securing internship roles at Ardhi SACCO and Goldenmax Solar Energy Limited, where I gained real-world exposure to credit analysis, financial modelling, and reporting.

Today, I am actively pursuing the Certified Investment and Financial Analyst (CIFA) qualification through KASNEB — sharpening my technical edge to deliver sophisticated investment analysis and financial strategies for the organizations I serve.

"My goal is to contribute to a dynamic finance team in East Africa and beyond — where I can apply analytical rigor to real-world problems and grow into a trusted investment or financial advisory role."
